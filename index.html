FAQ

How does “pending approval” work?
Enable “Requires confirmation” in your Cal.com event type settings. Requests come in pending; you approve from your phone/app.
Will Cal.com only show available slots?
Yes—Cal.com availability + calendar conflicts determine what customers can pick.
